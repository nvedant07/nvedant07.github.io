Hello! I'm Vedant!

I am a PhD student in the Computer Science Department at University of Maryland, College Park and MPI-SWS starting Fall 2019. I am a part of the Maryland-Max Planck joint program. My academic and research interests lie in topics related to Explainability, Transparency and Fairness in Machine Learning.

I did my undergrad in Computer Science and Engineering at IIIT, Delhi (2015-19), where I was associated to Precog, and worked in the field of Social Computing. During my undergrad I worked on topics related to Social Computing, Computational Social Science and ICT4D.

I am fortunate to have worked with the following amazing mentors: Ponnurangam Kumaraguru (PK), Pushpendra Singh (IIIT Delhi), Rijurekha Sen (IIT Delhi) and Hoda Heidari (ETH Zürich). I spent the wonderful summer of 2018 interning at MPI-SWS where I was advised by Krishna P. Gummadi.

For more, check out my CV.
